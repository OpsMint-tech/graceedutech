# Grace Edutech

Grace Edutech is a premier institution providing professional coaching and comprehensive financial compliance services since 2009. We specialize in academic excellence and business growth through expert mentorship and practical training.

## Core Services

### Coaching & Education
- **NET / SET Coaching**: Expert guidance for University and State level lecturership exams.
- **GATE Coaching**: Comprehensive preparation for Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering.
- **Spoken English**: Communication skills and confidence building.
- **Tally & Accounting**: Practical training in Tally Prime, GST, and Payroll.
- **E-Commerce Training**: Marketplace management for Amazon and Flipkart.

### Accounting & Compliance
- **Financial Accounting**: Bookkeeping, financial reporting, and record maintenance.
- **GST & Taxation**: GST filing, Income Tax returns, and tax planning.
- **Business Registrations**: Company incorporation (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), Trademark, and Shop Acts.
- **Professional Services**: Audit support, payroll management, and financial consulting.
